Our Curriculum

Our carefully crafted curriculum is designed to engage children in a wide range of age-appropriate activities that promote their physical, social, emotional, and cognitive development. From sensory play and creative arts to early literacy and math exploration, we offer a diverse range of educational experiences that ignite your child's curiosity and love for learning.

Safety and Hygiene

Our facility is equipped with modern security systems and adheres to strict safety protocols, ensuring a secure environment for your little one. We maintain clean and hygienic spaces, and our dedicated staff regularly undergoes training to stay updated on best practices in child care.

Parental Involvement

We understand that leaving your child in someone else's care can be a daunting decision. That is why we provide regular updates on your child's progress, including daily reports, photos, and ongoing communication. We want you to feel confident and assured that your child is in capable hands. We also encourage parental involvement through family events, workshops, and open-door policies. We value your input and strive to create a partnership that supports your child's growth and development.

Visit Us

Choosing Kirkland Child Care means choosing a nurturing and enriching environment where your child can flourish. We invite you to visit our facility, meet our dedicated team, and experience firsthand the love and care that sets us apart. Join us on this exciting journey of early childhood education, and together, let's lay the foundation for a bright future for your child.
