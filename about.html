Our Mission

Our passionate and dedicated team of caregivers and educators are committed to providing the highest quality of care for your little ones. With years of experience in early childhood education, we understand the importance of creating a safe, loving, and engaging space where children can explore, play, and develop essential skills.

We strive to foster a positive and inclusive environment that celebrates diversity, promoting understanding and respect for all children and families. Our staff undergoes continuous professional development to stay updated with the latest research and best practices in early childhood education, ensuring that your child receives the best possible care and educational experiences.

Our Philosophy

We embrace a child-centered approach that encourages curiosity, creativity, and independence. Our carefully designed curriculum is based on age-appropriate activities and educational resources that promote social, emotional, cognitive, and physical development. Through a balance of structured learning and free play, we foster a love for learning and help children develop a solid foundation for future success.

Our skilled educators provide a wide range of stimulating activities that support various areas of development. These activities may include arts and crafts, music and movement, storytime, outdoor play, and opportunities for imaginary play. We encourage children to express their thoughts and ideas, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

  • Provide a safe and nurturing environment for children.
  • Promote a love for learning through engaging activities and play.
  • Encourage social and emotional development through positive interactions.
  • Foster creativity and imagination.
  • Collaborate with parents to support the child's growth and development.
  • Offer nutritious meals and snacks to support healthy development.
  • Provide a structured schedule that includes learning, playtime, and rest periods.

Safety and Communication

Safety is paramount at kirkland child care. Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art security measures, including secure entry systems and surveillance cameras, ensuring that your child is always in a secure environment. Additionally, our staff members are trained in first aid and CPR, and we maintain rigorous health and hygiene protocols to safeguard the well-being of all children in our care.

We understand the importance of open and effective communication with parents and guardians. We provide regular updates on your child's progress, share memorable moments, and encourage your active involvement in your child's learning journey. We believe in building a strong partnership with families, as we recognize that parents are a child's first and most influential teachers.

Our dedicated staff members are always available to address any questions or concerns you may have. We encourage open dialogue and strive to create a supportive and collaborative relationship with parents, working together to provide the best possible care and education for your child.

Thank you for considering kirkland child care. We look forward to the opportunity of being part of your child's growth and development.
